Steak recipe

Description

The best steak in the world. I don't wanna be cocky, but I spent years of my life perfecting this recipe to ensure that it was the PERFECT recipe. Nothing beats this.

All the ingredients to be used for this recipe have to be fresh. By fresh, I mean literally taken within the day. For example, the oil has to be extracted from your in home farm (which I assume you have, as all good cooks and bakers do). The recipe will also call for a "cooking" portion in which I require you to use a cow costume while cooking as it lulls the beef into a false sense of security. At the end, you'll have the perfect plate of steak, enough to make Gordon Ramsey himself implode and then come back to life then explode, repeating an endless cycle of ecstacy and pain simply because of how mouth-wateringly delicious this recipe is. Good luck!

Ingredients

  • Gordon salt
  • New York Steak
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Rosemary
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Pat the steak dry to get a perect sear
  2. Season generously with any seasoning
  3. Preheat Pan
  4. Sear the steak
  5. Add butter, rosemary, onions
  6. Once done, remove the steak and let it rest
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Brownie Recipe

Description

The best brownies in the world. Not to toot my own horn, but I spent years of my life perfecting this recipe to ensure that it was the PERFECT recipe. Nothing beats this.

All the ingredients to be used for this recipe have to be fresh. By fresh, I mean literally taken within the day. For example, the oil has to be extracted from your in home farm (which I assume you have, as all good cooks and bakers do). The recipe will also call for a "baking" portion in which I require you to use an oven that cooks temperature perfectly up to 4 decimals of celcius. At the end, you'll have the perfect batch of brownies, enough to make Gordon Ramsey himself implode and then come back to life then explode, repeating an endless cycle of ecstacy and pain simply because of how mouth-wateringly delicious this recipe is. Good luck!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs + water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Oil
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. First, mix wet and dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  3. Pour into 8x8 baking pan with parchment paper
  4. Bake it!
  5. Explode!!!!

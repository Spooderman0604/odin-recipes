Corned Beef Recipe

Description

The best corned beef in the world. It's not my intention to boast, but I spent years of my life perfecting this recipe to ensure that it was the PERFECT recipe. Nothing beats this.

All the ingredients to be used for this recipe have to be fresh. By fresh, I mean literally taken within the day. For example, the oil has to be extracted from your in home farm (which I assume you have, as all good cooks and bakers do). The recipe will also call for a "frying" portion in which I require you to use purple fire specifically or you won't get the intended results (trust). At the end, you'll have the perfect serving of corned beef, enough to make Gordon Ramsey himself implode and then come back to life then explode, repeating an endless cycle of ecstacy and pain simply because of how mouth-wateringly delicious this recipe is. Good luck!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Corned beef
  • Chopped potatoes
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Egg

Steps

  1. First, fry the chopped potatoes then set them aside
  2. Next, cook the garlic until it browns
  3. Then, add the corned beef along with the potatoes
  4. Add the onions, then season to taste
  5. On the side, fry the egg
  6. Plate it with the egg on top and serve!
